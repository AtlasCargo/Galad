# Sub-state Source List (Initial, Neutral)

This list prioritizes open, primary sources suitable for a first pass.
It does not imply any ideological stance by listed organizations.

## Legal entity registries / identifiers
- Global Legal Entity Identifier Foundation (GLEIF) LEI data (open files)
- OpenCorporates (company registry aggregator; bulk access varies)
- Open Ownership (Beneficial Ownership Data Standard and registry tools)

## Company and financial filings (high budget/revenue entities)
- U.S. SEC EDGAR submissions (public companies)
- National corporate registries (e.g., Companies House UK)

## NGOs / charities / foundations
- IRS Exempt Organizations Business Master File (U.S.)
- UK Charity Commission register (UK)
- IATI Registry (international aid organizations and publishers)

## Universities and education systems
- National education registries (varies by country)
- University system public filings / annual reports

## Labor unions / professional associations
- National labor ministry registries (varies by country)
- Union confederation public reports

## Political parties
- V-Party (already ingested)

## Media groups
- Corporate registries + public financial filings
- National media authority registers (varies by country)

## Government agencies / SOEs
- National public sector registries / SOE lists
- Budget/annual report disclosures
